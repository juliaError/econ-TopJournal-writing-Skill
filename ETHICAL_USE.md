# Ethical Use Policy

This repository is intended for ethical economics writing support, research training, teaching, legitimate editing, self-revision, referee preparation, and transparent writing workflow improvement.

## Allowed Uses

You may use the original repository content for non-commercial purposes such as:

- learning economics paper structure and style;
- improving your own draft with proper authorship responsibility;
- teaching academic writing and revision;
- preparing legitimate referee reports or revision plans;
- building non-commercial research-writing workflows with attribution.

## Prohibited Uses

Do not use this repository, its original skills, or its derived workflows for:

- paid paper-writing services;
- ghostwriting papers, theses, grant applications, referee responses, or coursework for undisclosed authorship;
- academic misconduct services;
- fabricating data, results, citations, tables, figures, or literature claims;
- disguising AI-generated text as independent human authorship where disclosure is required;
- helping users evade journal, university, or funder rules;
- commercial products or services without separate written permission for the original repository content.

## Authorship Responsibility

Users remain responsible for the accuracy, originality, citations, disclosure choices, and ethical compliance of any manuscript or research output produced with help from these skills.
